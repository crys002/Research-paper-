Research Overview

Why This Matters

Mental health disorders affect 1 in 4 people globally — early detection can save lives.

🔍

Problem Statement

Depression, anxiety, and suicidal ideation are severely underdiagnosed. Social media platforms contain valuable early warning signals that can be detected using AI.

⚙️

Methodology

We benchmark 5 state-of-the-art NLP architectures — from traditional BiLSTM to domain-specialized MentalBERT — across two real-world mental health datasets.

📦

Datasets Used

CLPsych 2015: 1,146 ReachOut.com posts (forum peer support). RSDD: 18,400 Reddit posts from mental health subreddits. Both binary classified.

🎯

Key Finding

MentalBERT (domain-pretrained on mental health text) consistently outperforms general-purpose transformers, proving that domain adaptation is critical in clinical NLP.
